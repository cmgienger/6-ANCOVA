Can also use the ‘ancovaplot’ function in the HH package to plot and fit lines

ancovaplot(EGGS ~ DENSITY + SEASON, data=limp) #equal slopes plot

ancovaplot(EGGS ~ DENSITY * SEASON, data=limp) #separate slopes plot
